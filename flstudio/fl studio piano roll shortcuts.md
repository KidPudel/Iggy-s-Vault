piano roll shortcuts
- cmd b: duplicate
- cmd g: glue that notes that are together
- cmd l: extend note till next
- shift d: reduce length of notes
- shift l: auto look up/extend the length of the note until the next one
- cmd q: extend node to the proper seciton
- cmd e: step input recording
- opt k: piano roll limiter to certain style
- shift + arrows: shifts notes by a note
- cmd + arrows: shift notes by octaves
- ctrl+opt+click: play sound
- cmd+select: selects the notes
	- tip: while selected, the stretch the note arrow appears
- shift+drag selected: duplicates the note
- double click on a note: change property of a note
